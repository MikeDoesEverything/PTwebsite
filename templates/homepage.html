What separates me from other PT’s?


I am the most qualified personal trainer in the area. With my bachelor’s degree in Sports Science, level 4 in strength and conditioning and level 3 in personal training and my other numerous qualifications, there is no competition. And even more importantly, I am still building upon my knowledge and skills in order to provide you with the best service. In my experience personal training, I have worked with clients of all shapes sizes and skill levels. Some of my clients were obese, some were anorexic, some worked in the military, some were elite, world class athletes, some were bodybuilders and some spent too much time behind a desk and just wanted some exercise. Whatever your goals, I can help you achieve them.

Afraid of starting? Don’t be

I realise that many of you will be newcomers to fitness. I understand you will likely feel intimidated, as this is likely all new territory to you. If you do feel this way please feel free to call me, or message me on 07964237996. I provide a free, no obligation 30 minute consultation, to any new customer. This can be done either in person or over the phone. But if the idea of calling me makes you anxious, please feel free to text me on the number above, or email theathleteapproach@gmail.com You really have nothing to lose. So if you feel that you want guidance or even if you’re not sure, please contact me.



My commitment to you

Each client has individual needs. And I am committed to ensuring that whatever your goal, you get results. I have had clients in the past who had serious health issues, previous devastating injuries, or anxiety issues. In all cases I am committed to doing the research, so I can accommodate your needs, and, craft and implement the ideal training plan to work around those needs.
